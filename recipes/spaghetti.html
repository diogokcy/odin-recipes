Spaghetti with Tomato Sauce

Description

This classic Spaghetti with Tomato Sauce is a simple, comforting dish that's perfect for weeknight dinners or lazy Sunday meals. Made with pantry staples like garlic, onion, and canned tomatoes, it delivers rich flavor with minimal effort. The sauce is gently simmered to bring out the natural sweetness of the tomatoes, while garlic and olive oil add depth and aroma.

Whether you're cooking for one or feeding a family, this recipe is both satisfying and versatile. Add fresh basil and grated Parmesan for a finishing touch, or customize it with your favorite vegetables or protein. It's a timeless meal that never goes out of style.

Ingredients

  • 400g (14 oz) of spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 can (400g / 14 oz) of crushed tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar (optional, to reduce acidity)
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta:
    • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
    • Add the spaghetti and cook according to the package instructions until al dente (firm to the bite).
    • Drain and set aside.
  2. Make the sauce:
    • In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
    • Add the chopped onion and cook for 3–4 minutes until soft.
    • Add the garlic and sauté for 1 minute until fragrant.
    • Pour in the crushed tomatoes.
    • Add salt, pepper, and sugar (if using).
    • Let the sauce simmer for about 10–15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Combine:
    • Add the drained spaghetti to the sauce.
    • Toss well to coat the pasta evenly.
  4. Serve:
    • Serve hot, topped with fresh basil and grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
